What is the OER World Map?

The OER World Map is the place on the web where anyone can expand their knowledge, share information and experiences and collect data on OER initiatives.

Anyone can access, contribute to and collect the information on the World Map.

Our mission

We aim to map OER initiatives around the world to facilitate the expansion of knowledge and the exchange of information, experience and data between individuals and communities.

We believe that Open Education has the potential to enhance education for all, to foster democratic participation, sustainable development and social justice. Helping others to open and transform education, its institutions, practices and resources is what gets us going every morning.
